About this session

Keep creating opportunities beyond your current market.

Markets shift, and the salespeople who keep winning are the ones who introduce new ideas, challenge assumptions, and keep creating opportunities. This session is a practical look at how to do exactly that.

What to expect

On-time delivery, practical insights backed by real examples, interactive audience polls, and a live Q&A with Nadim. We’ll also hand a prize to the most engaged participant.

Who it’s for

Business owners and founders, sales leaders, sales executives, and business development professionals who want to grow beyond the accounts and markets they already serve.

What you’ll leave with

How to spot the limits of relying on existing relationships, create new opportunities, adapt your approach to new buyers, communicate value clearly, and build the growth mindset that keeps a pipeline full.
