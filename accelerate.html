The 90-day program

Three stages, from diagnosis to sustained performance.

01 Weeks 1–3

Diagnose

Assess and set direction
  • Review sales performance and pipeline health
  • Gauge individual strengths, team dynamics, and sales effectiveness
  • Set sales targets and success measures
  • Develop an action plan
02 Weeks 4–9

Execute

Train and apply
  • One day of sales training per week for six weeks
  • Covers the full sales cycle, from prospecting to closing
  • Practical tools and frameworks applied to live deals
  • Application, reflection, and accountability between sessions
03 Weeks 10–12

Accelerate

Review and sustain
  • Review progress against objectives
  • Evaluate improvements and key learnings
  • Provide a summary report and recommendations
  • Align on next steps and future development priorities

Ongoing support (optional): two days of training, coaching, or facilitation each month to reinforce learning, address new challenges, and sustain sales performance.
