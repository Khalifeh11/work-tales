The 90-day program

Three stages, from diagnosis to sustained performance.

Weeks 1–3

Diagnose

Assess and set direction
  • Review sales performance and pipeline health
  • Gauge individual strengths, team dynamics, and sales effectiveness
  • Set sales targets and success measures
  • Develop an action plan
Weeks 4–9

Execute

Train and apply
  • One day of sales training per week for six weeks
  • Covers the full sales cycle, from prospecting to closing
  • Practical tools and frameworks applied to live deals
  • Application, reflection, and accountability between sessions
Weeks 10–12

Accelerate

Review and sustain
  • Review progress against objectives
  • Evaluate improvements and key learnings
  • Provide a summary report and recommendations
  • Align on next steps and future development priorities

Ongoing support

Optional

Two days of training, coaching, or facilitation each month to reinforce learning, address new challenges, and sustain sales performance.

Our Insights Discovery partnership

A shared language for how people think, work, and connect.

The Diagnose stage is grounded in Insights Discovery, a psychometric tool that helps people better understand themselves and others.

Using a simple four-colour model, it provides insight into communication styles, strengths, motivations, and how individuals interact with others.

The result is a shared language that improves communication, collaboration, and team effectiveness.

Cool Blue

cautious · precise · deliberate · questioning · formal

Fiery Red

competitive · demanding · determined · strong-willed · purposeful

Earth Green

Sunshine Yellow
