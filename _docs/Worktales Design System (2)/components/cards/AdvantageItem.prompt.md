Compact feature card — icon tile, heading, short paragraph. Used for "why hire with us" and the Lebanese-advantage grids.

```jsx
<AdvantageItem
  icon="fa-microchip"
  title="Modern talent sourcing"
  description="AI-assisted sourcing and structured assessment surface the right candidates faster."
/>
```

Flat (no hover lift) by design — sits in the dark with a hairline border. Lay out with `repeat(auto-fit, minmax(230px, 1fr))`.
